四川艺站画室

四川艺站画室成立于2007年，（原名：刘老师画室），有着丰富的教学经验，专业美术老师授课，本着因材施教的教学理念，将个体教育导入 的原则，注重创造及贤能的开发，并坚持逐个培养，小班授课的教学形式，在这里，让您的孩子学会欣赏美，发现美，创造美，以得到全面的发展与提高。

    Randy and Erin shot our wedding in August 2012 in Portland, OR. This duo seriously knows how to capture people’s joy. Not only did they capture how ridiculously happy we were that day, they effectively captured this wonderful strange city we live in. As the cliche goes, our wedding day went by so fast and seemed like such a blur. But to have an everlasting testament of the amazingness of that day is such a gift. My wife and I still look at the album regularly because their work brings us back to that warm and fuzzy place. Above all, these guys are pros and will get the best out your special day.
